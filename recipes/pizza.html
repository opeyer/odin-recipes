Pizza

Description

This fantastic homemade naan pizza will perk you up and creates lots of leftovers.

You won't regret taking the time to make this delicious naan pizza!

Ingredients

  • Naan
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • OPTIONAL: Toppings

Steps

  1. Make the pizza sauce by simmering tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, basil, and a bit of sugar together until thick and delicious, or purchase a jar of store-bought sauce.
  2. Assemble the pizzas.
  3. Bake until the edges are toasty and cheese is melty.
  4. Slice and enjoy.

This recipe taken from Baking Mischief.
